I am a PhD student in Psychological and Brain Sciences at UC Santa Barbara, working in the Vision and Image Understanding Lab under the mentorship of Dr. Miguel Eckstein.

My research focuses on understanding how the human visual system extracts meaning from complex, naturalistic scenes. I combine eye tracking methodologies with computational approaches to investigate the mechanisms underlying visual perception and scene understanding.
